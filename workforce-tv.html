About Workforce TV

Workforce TV is a live discussion series hosted by Government Workforce Network, designed to create direct access between employers and individuals exploring career opportunities.

These sessions provide real conversations—not just presentations—where participants can hear from professionals, ask questions, and gain insight into the realities of different careers.

This is part of our mission to build income, opportunity, and well-being by creating accessible, real-world workforce connections.

For Students & Participants

Workforce TV provides a space to learn directly from employers, understand career expectations, and explore opportunities in a way that is accessible and engaging.

  • Ask questions directly to professionals
  • Learn about real job responsibilities
  • Understand hiring expectations
  • Explore career pathways

For Employers & Organizations

Organizations can participate as featured guests in Workforce TV sessions, providing visibility, engaging with talent, and sharing insights about career opportunities.

Workforce TV is part of our employer engagement model and may be included as part of partnership or service packages. Request pricing and details to learn more about availability and package options.
