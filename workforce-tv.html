About Workforce TV

Workforce TV is a workforce engagement experience hosted by Government Workforce Network LLC. It is designed for employers, institutions, government agencies, nonprofits, and private-sector organizations seeking a more strategic way to connect with diverse talent communities.

Through live sessions hosted on Microsoft Teams, organizations can share career insight, discuss hiring needs, highlight mission and culture, elevate visibility, and engage directly with intended audiences in a professional and interactive environment.

Rather than relying solely on traditional outreach, Workforce TV offers a more elevated format for conversation, workforce visibility, and authentic connection.

Workforce TV supports Government Workforce Network LLC’s mission to build income, opportunity, and well-being by creating accessible, meaningful, and real-world workforce connections.

Who Workforce TV Serves

Students & Emerging Talent

Learn directly from employers, gain career exposure, and better understand workplace expectations and opportunities.

Professionals & Job Seekers

Explore career pathways, hear from industry leaders, and access insight that supports growth and transition.

Veterans & Diverse Communities

Support broader workforce inclusion by creating accessible opportunities for veterans, military-connected individuals, retirees, individuals with disabilities, and other communities.

For Participants

Workforce TV gives participants a more direct and meaningful way to engage with employers and organizations. Sessions are designed to be informative, accessible, and focused on real workforce value.

  • Ask thoughtful questions directly to professionals and organizational representatives
  • Gain insight into real roles, responsibilities, and workplace expectations
  • Learn about hiring pathways, culture, and career preparation
  • Access workforce conversations in a convenient virtual format
Workforce TV is intended to inform, engage, and connect audiences with real workforce insight. Participation in a session does not guarantee employment, placement, or selection.

For Employers & Organizations

Workforce TV is a premium employer engagement offering for organizations seeking to connect with targeted audiences through curated live sessions hosted by Government Workforce Network LLC.

These sessions can be structured around employer spotlights, recruitment discussions, hiring conversations, workforce awareness, program visibility, community engagement, or educational career exposure.

Through Workforce TV, your organization can present its mission, culture, priorities, opportunities, and industry perspective while engaging directly with students, professionals, veterans, military-connected individuals, retirees, individuals with disabilities, job seekers, and diverse communities.

Each session is designed to support visibility, elevate employer branding, and create more authentic dialogue through real-time interaction.

Customized packages are available for government agencies, educational institutions, nonprofits, workforce development organizations, and private-sector employers based on your goals, format, and intended audience.
