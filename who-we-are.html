Our Foundation

At Government Workforce Network, people come first. Our work is centered on building income, opportunity, and well-being.

We believe workforce systems should recognize potential, not overlook it. Visibility, access, innovation, and human-centered solutions are at the core of how we build programs, partnerships, and workforce engagement experiences.

Workforce Innovation Lab

Government Workforce Network believes meaningful workforce innovation should be built on more than ideas. It should be supported by evidence, continuous learning, measurable outcomes, and a commitment to lasting public benefit.

As we develop AI workforce tools, workforce initiatives, and solutions, we document our progress through foundation reports, case studies, pilot programs, research briefs, and annual workforce innovation reports. These publications capture the workforce challenges we identify, the solutions we build, the lessons we learn, and the measurable outcomes that guide continuous improvement.

Every initiative we develop is evaluated through our three foundational pillars: Income. Opportunity. Well-Being.

Together, these pillars guide how we design, test, evaluate, and continuously improve every workforce solution we create.

Our Commitment

Government Workforce Network is building practical AI workforce solutions designed to complement—not replace—the valuable work already being performed by employers, educational institutions, workforce organizations, and public agencies. Our mission is to help bridge emerging workforce gaps by developing practical, accessible, efficient, and forward-thinking resources that support hiring, career development, workforce readiness, and economic opportunity in an AI-driven future.

As part of this commitment, Talent Connect-USA provides participants with free AI workforce resources designed to help individuals strengthen their skills, discover new opportunities, and confidently navigate the changing world of work. These free AI workforce resources are designed to support income, opportunity, and well-being while documenting what works, what needs improvement, and how workforce innovation can create lasting public benefit.

As our work evolves, we will continue documenting our progress through transparent research, pilot initiatives, and evidence-based workforce innovation to help strengthen opportunity for future generations.
