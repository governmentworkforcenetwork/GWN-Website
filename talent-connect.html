Human-Centered Access in an AI-Driven Hiring Environment

Talent Connect

Talent Connect is a workforce visibility and connection program designed to help bridge the growing gap between people and opportunity in a hiring environment increasingly shaped by automation, screening filters, and reduced human interaction.

As hiring continues to evolve, many qualified individuals are filtered out before being seen. Talent Connect restores access by creating more intentional pathways between talent and opportunity helping people gain visibility, strengthen workforce readiness, and move forward with greater clarity.

About Talent Connect

Talent Connect is built to strengthen real human connection in a workforce increasingly influenced by automation.

While AI may improve efficiency, it can also reduce meaningful interaction and create new barriers for individuals seeking opportunity and direction. Talent Connect reintroduces access, visibility, and conversation into the experience helping individuals become seen beyond a resume while supporting more intentional workforce connection.

By integrating workforce visibility with AI readiness awareness, Talent Connect supports a more informed and human-centered approach to opportunity, engagement, and hiring.
