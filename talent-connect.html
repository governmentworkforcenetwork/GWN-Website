About Talent Connect

A more human-centered path to opportunity

Talent Connect is designed specifically for job seekers who want stronger workforce visibility, meaningful support, and a more accessible way to move forward in a rapidly evolving hiring landscape.

This is not built around gatekeeping. It is a free program intended to reduce barriers and help individuals better navigate a workforce environment increasingly shaped by automation and AI screening.

Participants may also be considered for specialized tools and resources based on their background, interests, and career direction.

Free to join. Minimal information collected. No resume required to get started. Everyone is welcome.
