A Place for Talent to Be Seen

Talent Connect-USA is designed for people who need workforce resources, career support, visibility, and connection in today’s hiring environment.

Many qualified people are applying online but not being seen. Talent Connect-USA helps create a more human-centered way to identify available talent and connect people to resources, updates, events, and employer visibility opportunities.

U.S. Access Is Free

No Cost for U.S. Participants

Talent Connect-USA is free for individuals located in the United States. Participants may join at no cost to receive Talent Connect related communication, workforce resources, event updates, and visibility opportunities.

What does free mean?

Free means there is no cost for U.S. participants to join Talent Connect-USA. No hidden fees. No required subscription. No account setup required to submit the form.
