Why This Structure Matters

Each part has a purpose.

Talent brings real workforce needs. Engagement helps us understand those needs. Research and Innovation document what we learn. Solutions and Opportunities help turn that learning into practical workforce action.

Together, this structure keeps Government Workforce Network focused on building pathways to income, expanding opportunity, and promoting well-being.

Operating Model

From real needs to real solutions.

We do not build from assumptions. We listen, identify gaps, build tools, test ideas, and improve based on what people and partners actually need.

01

Listen

Learn from students, workers, employers, universities, and communities.

02

Identify

Define workforce barriers affecting access, readiness, opportunity, and well-being.

03

Build

Create practical AI-enabled workforce tools, programs, and resources.

04

Test

Use pilots, feedback, and engagement to learn what works and what needs improvement.

05

Improve

Document lessons learned and strengthen the next version of our work.
