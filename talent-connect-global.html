A Different Pathway for International Talent

Talent Connect Global was created for individuals outside the United States who want access to workforce resources, professional visibility support, and connection in a global workforce environment.

This is separate from Talent Connect USA. Talent Connect Global is a membership-based access path for international participants.

Membership Access

Talent Connect Global Membership

Membership gives international participants access to Talent Connect Global communications, resources, workforce visibility support, and global career connection opportunities as they become available.

Workforce Resources

Receive career guidance, workforce updates, tools, and information designed for global talent.

Visibility Support

Become part of a global talent community built to strengthen professional visibility and connection.

Portal Access

After payment is received, members receive access instructions for the Talent Connect Global portal.

Important Membership Note

Talent Connect Global membership does not guarantee employment, interview selection, placement, visa sponsorship, employer response, or job offer. The program provides workforce visibility, resources, communication, and connection support.
