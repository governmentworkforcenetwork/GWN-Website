Employer Talent Request

A workforce access solution designed to help employers, organizations, and workforce partners connect with a broader, more intentional pipeline of job seekers through visibility, outreach, and human-centered engagement.

Access Talent • Expand Reach • Hire with Intention

Employer Talent Request is designed for organizations seeking stronger access to talent beyond traditional recruiting channels. In today’s hiring environment, automation can limit visibility and reduce meaningful connection between employers and qualified individuals.

This request process helps organizations strengthen outreach, expand access to workforce communities, and engage with a broader pool of job seekers through a more intentional, human-centered pathway.

Important: Submission of an employer talent request does not guarantee candidate placement, immediate referrals, or hiring outcomes. It is intended to support access, visibility, and workforce connection.

What Employer Talent Request Offers

Broader Talent Access

Connect with job seekers across workforce networks, community-based outreach, universities, and other talent-connected pathways.

Intentional Outreach

Expand your reach beyond automated systems and create more direct opportunities for workforce engagement and visibility.

Human-Centered Hiring Support

Strengthen recruitment efforts through connection-focused strategies that support access, awareness, and participation.

AI-Aware Workforce Strategy

Balance evolving recruitment technology with more meaningful, people-centered pathways to talent discovery and engagement.

Who This Is For

  • Employers seeking stronger access to job seekers
  • Organizations looking to expand outreach and visibility
  • Public sector, nonprofit, and private sector employers
  • Teams seeking more inclusive and intentional workforce engagement
  • Organizations interested in connecting with diverse workforce communities
  • Employers looking for more human-centered hiring pathways

How the Process Works

  1. Submit the Request Form — Complete the employer talent request form with your organization’s information and workforce needs.
  2. Request Review — The information submitted is reviewed to better understand your hiring interests, outreach goals, and areas of workforce connection.
  3. Workforce Visibility Support — Your request may be used to inform future outreach, engagement opportunities, employer sessions, or related workforce access efforts.
  4. Connection Opportunities — When aligned, your organization may be connected to relevant talent engagement activities, visibility opportunities, or workforce-related communication.

What to Expect After You Submit

Your request helps us better understand your organization’s workforce interests and areas of talent need.

You may receive communication related to workforce engagement opportunities, relevant sessions, outreach efforts, or broader talent visibility pathways connected to Talent Connect.

Employer Talent Request is intended to support stronger access, communication, and workforce connection. It does not replace your internal hiring process or guarantee placement outcomes.
