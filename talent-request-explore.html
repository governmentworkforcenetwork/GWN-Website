Talent Connect – Employer Request

A workforce access solution designed to help employers, organizations, and workforce partners connect with a broader, more intentional pipeline of job seekers through visibility, outreach, and human-centered engagement.

Access Talent • Expand Reach • Hire with Intention

What This Is

Talent Connect – Employer Request is designed for organizations seeking stronger access to talent beyond traditional recruiting channels. In today’s hiring environment, automation can limit visibility and reduce meaningful connection between employers and qualified individuals.

This service helps organizations strengthen outreach, expand access to workforce communities, and engage with a broader pool of job seekers through a more intentional, human-centered approach.

What This Offers

Broader Talent Access

Connect with job seekers across workforce networks, outreach channels, community pathways, and other visibility-centered opportunities.

Intentional Outreach

Expand your reach beyond automated systems and create stronger visibility for workforce engagement and talent discovery.

Human-Centered Hiring Support

Support access, awareness, and participation through connection-focused workforce strategies designed to enhance visibility and engagement.

AI-Aware Workforce Strategy

Balance evolving recruitment technology with more meaningful, people-centered pathways to talent connection and workforce access.

Who This Is For

  • Employers seeking stronger access to job seekers
  • Organizations looking to expand outreach and visibility
  • Public sector, nonprofit, and private sector employers
  • Teams seeking more inclusive and intentional workforce engagement
  • Organizations interested in connecting with diverse workforce communities
  • Employers looking for more human-centered hiring pathways

How the Process Works

  1. Submit the Form — Complete the employer request form with your organization’s information and workforce needs.
  2. Initial Review — We review your submission to better understand your hiring goals, outreach priorities, and areas of support.
  3. Follow-Up Discussion — If appropriate, we may contact you to discuss your needs in more detail and identify the most suitable service approach.
  4. Scope and Pricing — Based on your organization’s needs, we may discuss package options, service scope, and pricing details.
  5. Next Steps — If there is alignment, we will share the next steps for moving forward.

What to Expect

  • Confirmation that your request was submitted
  • Review of your workforce interests and hiring needs
  • Possible follow-up communication to better understand your goals
  • Discussion of service scope, support options, and recommended package
  • Pricing details based on your organization’s needs
  • Next steps shared if there is alignment to move forward

Important Information

Employer Request is a paid service. Submitting a request form does not create a contract, guarantee immediate service, or confirm pricing.

After your request is received, we may contact you to better understand your workforce needs, discuss service scope, and determine the most appropriate support approach, package, and pricing details.

Services begin only after scope, pricing, and next steps are reviewed and agreed upon.

Please note: Talent Connect – Employer Request is designed to support stronger workforce access, communication, visibility, and engagement. It does not replace your internal hiring process and does not guarantee candidate placement, immediate referrals, or hiring outcomes.
