Together, Protecting and Promoting Employee Well-Being

Lead With Who You Serve

Founded by a retired public servant in 2025, the Government Workforce Network unites people who’ve dedicated their lives to public service. We support government employees, military members, and veterans with connection, compassion, and career growth—beyond ranks, titles, or boundaries.

Mission

To build a global, rank-free community for current and former government employees and military members — a trusted space where well-being, mentorship, leadership, and workforce development come first.

Vision

A world where every public servant — from clerks to directors, from new recruits to retirees — feels supported, valued, and empowered to thrive. Leadership, mentorship, and well-being are not privileges of rank, but rights of every employee in public service.

Glow + Grow Initiative

Empowerment, Connection, and Purpose. A signature series celebrating service and growth through empowerment circles, mixers, and recognition galas — blending learning with inspiration and joy.
