What Is the Career Connection Festival?

The Career Connection Festival is a virtual workforce engagement experience designed to give each employer a dedicated branded lounge where attendees can learn about the organization, explore career opportunities, access employer resources, and join live engagement spaces.

🏢

Dedicated Employer Lounge

Each participating employer receives a virtual lounge designed to showcase who they are, what they offer, and how attendees can connect with them.

💼

Flexible Lounge Format

Employers may use their lounge as a recruiting space, meet-and-greet room, or company showcase depending on their workforce goals.

🚪

Easy Attendee Access

Students and job seekers can browse employer lounges and join the spaces that match their career goals, interests, and industry focus.

After Registration

After payment confirmation, employers will receive a follow-up confirmation email with next steps. Employers will select one of three lounge experiences and submit their logo, organization profile, website links, career links, hiring focus, and optional resources.

💼

Recruiting Lounge

Best for employers actively promoting current openings, internships, apprenticeships, entry-level opportunities, or future hiring needs.

🤝

Meet & Greet Lounge

Best for employers who want to build awareness, answer questions, and introduce their organization to future talent.

🎤

Company Showcase Lounge

Best for employers who want to highlight their mission, culture, benefits, hiring process, services, and career pathways.
