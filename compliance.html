At Government Workforce Network LLC, we believe workforce visibility and opportunity should be accessible to everyone. We are committed to operating in alignment with applicable laws, standards, and best practices that support accessibility, privacy, nondiscrimination, education collaboration, responsible technology, and public-sector readiness.

Accessibility

We are committed to making our platform usable by everyone, including individuals with disabilities.

  • Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA): We strive to design our digital experiences in alignment with ADA accessibility principles.
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1): We work toward meeting WCAG 2.1 standards for navigation, readability, and assistive technology compatibility.
  • Section 504: We recognize the importance of equal access and nondiscrimination for individuals with disabilities in education and federally supported programs.
  • Section 508: As an organization registered with SAM.gov and engaged with public-sector partners, we are aware of and work toward Section 508 federal accessibility standards for digital content.
If you experience any difficulty accessing content on this site, please contact us at admin@governmentworkforcenetwork.com so we can assist you.

Data Privacy

We respect the privacy of every student, professional, veteran, employer, and community member who engages with our programs.

  • CCPA / CPRA: As a California-based organization, we recognize and support the privacy rights of California residents, including the right to know, access, and request deletion of personal information.
  • COPPA: Our platform is not directed at, nor knowingly used to collect information from, children under the age of 13.
  • FERPA Awareness: When collaborating with educational institutions, we recognize the importance of student privacy and respect applicable student information protections.

For full details on how we collect, use, and protect personal information, please see our Privacy Policy.

Education & Workforce Development Alignment

Government Workforce Network LLC supports collaboration with universities, community colleges, workforce agencies, employers, veterans organizations, and community partners. Our programs are informed by recognized workforce-development and career-readiness principles.

  • FERPA Student Privacy Awareness: We recognize the importance of safeguarding student information when working with educational institutions.
  • NACE Career Readiness Principles: We support career readiness, ethical recruiting, and workforce-development practices consistent with guidance commonly used by higher education career services and employer relations professionals.
  • Career Readiness Competencies: We value communication, leadership, teamwork, professionalism, technology, critical thinking, equity, inclusion, and career preparation.
  • Veteran & Service Member Workforce Transition: We support efforts that help veterans, military spouses, reservists, and transitioning service members connect with civilian workforce opportunities.
  • Accessible Program Design: We aim to design programs and digital experiences that support participation from students, professionals, veterans, and community members with diverse needs.
Government Workforce Network LLC is not an accrediting body or educational institution. Our role is to support visibility, career readiness, employer engagement, workforce connection, and equitable access to opportunity.

Equal Opportunity & Nondiscrimination

We are committed to supporting fair and equitable access to workforce opportunity for all individuals.

  • Title VII of the Civil Rights Act: We support nondiscrimination across our programs and partnerships.
  • EEOC Guidance: We align employer-engagement and visibility programs with principles of fair access and nondiscrimination.
  • USERRA: We recognize and support the employment rights of veterans and service members transitioning into civilian careers.

Responsible Technology & AI Use

Government Workforce Network LLC may use emerging technologies, including artificial intelligence tools, to support content development, operational efficiency, workforce engagement, and program delivery.

We are committed to responsible, transparent, and ethical use of technology. We do not use AI as a substitute for human judgment in decisions affecting individuals. We continue to evaluate our technology practices to support fairness, accessibility, privacy, and accountability.

Federal & Public-Sector Standards

Government Workforce Network LLC is registered with SAM.gov UEI: L8PDM1AKWB89 and is committed to operating in alignment with applicable federal acquisition, contracting, and workforce program standards as we engage with public-sector partners.

  • Federal Acquisition Regulation Awareness: We recognize the importance of public-sector procurement standards when engaging with government partners.
  • Public-Sector Program Readiness: We strive to maintain responsible business, documentation, accessibility, privacy, and operational practices that support future government, education, and workforce partnerships.

Our Ongoing Commitment

Compliance and accessibility are not one-time efforts. They are ongoing commitments. We continually review our programs, platform, and partnerships to ensure they reflect current legal standards, public-sector expectations, education-sector expectations, and best practices.
