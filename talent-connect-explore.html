Talent Connect – Job Seekers

A visibility and access platform designed to help job seekers connect with employers, organizations, and workforce opportunities through intentional, human-centered pathways.

Be Seen • Be Considered • Be Connected

What This Is

Talent Connect is designed for job seekers who want greater visibility beyond traditional applications. In today’s AI-driven hiring environment, many qualified individuals are filtered out before they are fully seen.

Talent Connect helps create more direct pathways to opportunity by increasing visibility, expanding access, and supporting meaningful workforce connection through participating employers, workforce partners, and community-based engagement.

What This Offers

Increased Visibility

Gain exposure beyond traditional application systems and automated screening.

Workforce Access

Connect with workforce sessions, employer engagement, and opportunity-related pathways.

Inclusive Participation

Open to students, veterans, stay-at-home parents, career changers, returning professionals, and more.

AI-Aware Environment

Navigate a changing workforce landscape while still having room for meaningful human connection.

Who This Is For

  • Job seekers looking for greater visibility
  • Individuals impacted by automated application systems
  • Career changers and returning professionals
  • Students and early-career individuals
  • Veterans, military-connected individuals, and individuals with disabilities
  • Anyone seeking a more direct pathway to workforce access and opportunity

How the Process Works

  1. Submit the Form — Complete the Talent Connect form with your information.
  2. Join the Network — Your submission may be added to the Talent Connect participant list.
  3. Receive Updates — You may be informed about events, sessions, and workforce-related opportunities.
  4. Stay Connected — When relevant opportunities arise, you may receive information about openings or engagement activities.

What to Expect

  • Confirmation that your information was submitted
  • Possible addition to the Talent Connect participant list
  • Potential invitations to employer or workforce-related sessions
  • Updates on relevant opportunities or openings when available
  • A human-centered approach focused on visibility, connection, and access

Important Information

Participation is free. Submission does not guarantee employment, interview selection, placement, or direct employer response. Talent Connect is intended to increase visibility and strengthen access to workforce opportunities through connection, communication, and community-based engagement.

The future of workforce is not just automated.

It is connected, visible, and human.
